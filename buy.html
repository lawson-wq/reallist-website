Buying journey

Know the steps before the stress starts.

1. Finance ready

Bank approval tells you what you may borrow. It does not tell you what you should borrow.

2. Buying structure

Investors should discuss tax and ownership structure before buying, not after the numbers hurt.

3. Search criteria

Define area, budget, bedrooms, school zone, risk tolerance and deal-breakers.

4. Due diligence

Check title, LIM, property file, consent history, insurance, inspection and risk.

5. Offer

A rushed offer is not a strong offer. A clear offer is.

6. Settlement

Confirm insurance, finance, solicitor review, timing and post-purchase management.
