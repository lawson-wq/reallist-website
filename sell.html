The Reallist seller journey

A clear process beats vague promises.

01

Property Review

We review the property, ownership structure, available documents, rental potential and realistic market position.

02

Set a Real Asking Price

A high price is not a strategy. A real price is. We help set a clear asking price based on market evidence, not wishful thinking.

03

Build the Listing

We create a clean Reallist page with photos, key facts, Council CV, request options, rent appraisal, school zones and buyer actions.

04

Launch to Serious Buyers

Buyers request documents, nearby sales, rental information, private viewings or offer guidance directly from the property page.

05

Track Buyer Feedback

Seller feedback is based on actual buyer behaviour, not vague comments like “there is interest”.

06

Review Price and Offers

If the market does not respond, we review the price with evidence. If a buyer is ready, offers are handled through Climber Real Estate.

07

Sold, Reported, Recorded

Once sold, the result can become a transparent case study where appropriate, helping future sellers understand the market more clearly.

Why no default open homes?

Reallist does not rely on empty open homes. Buyers submit specific requests, ask real questions and leave trackable signals before private viewings are arranged.

Less random foot traffic. More serious buyer intent. Finally, a small victory for everyone’s calendar.
