Privacy Policy

Reallist collects enquiry information so we can respond to buyer, seller and developer requests. This may include your name, email, phone number, property interest, buyer type, budget range and message.

We use this information to respond to enquiries, arrange viewings, provide property information, record buyer requests and improve our service. We do not sell personal information to advertisers.

Property sale and offer processes are handled by licensed real estate professionals through Climber Real Estate. Information may be shared where required to respond to your enquiry, comply with legal obligations or progress a property transaction.

To request access, correction or deletion of your information, contact lawson@climberproperty.com.
